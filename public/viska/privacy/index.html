Viska — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Viska (“Viska”, “the app”, “we”, “us”), a word-guessing party game, handles your information. We designed Viska to be privacy-friendly: the core game is fully playable as a guest, offline, without an account. By using Viska you agree to the practices described below.

1. Play without an account

You can play Viska without signing in. In guest mode the game runs locally on your device and we do not collect personal information about you. Creating an account is optional and unlocks cloud sync, game history, and premium features.

2. Information we collect

We collect only what is needed to provide the features you use:

  • Account information. If you sign in with Email, Apple, or Google, we receive an account identifier and (depending on the method) your email address. Authentication is handled by our backend provider, Supabase. We use Sign in with Apple’s private-relay email when you choose to hide your address.
  • Your content. When signed in, the decks you create with the AI generator and your game history are stored in your account so they sync across devices.
  • Purchase information. If you buy a subscription or credit pack, our payments provider, RevenueCat, processes the transaction and stores your subscription status and transaction identifiers. We never receive or store your full payment-card details — those are handled entirely by Apple.
  • AI deck topics. When you use the “make any deck” feature, the topic text you enter is sent to our server and to Anthropic (the Claude API) to generate the deck. Don’t include personal or sensitive information in deck topics.
  • Language & settings. Your chosen language and app preferences are stored locally on your device.

3. How we use information

  • To provide and operate the game and its features.
  • To authenticate you and sync your content across devices.
  • To process and verify subscriptions and credit purchases.
  • To generate custom decks you request through the AI feature.
  • To maintain the security and reliability of the service.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use it for advertising.

4. Service providers

We rely on the following processors, who handle data on our behalf:

  • Supabase — authentication and database storage (accounts, custom decks, game history).
  • RevenueCat — subscription and in-app purchase management.
  • Apple — App Store payments and, if used, Sign in with Apple.
  • Google — if you choose Google sign-in.
  • Anthropic (Claude API) — generating AI decks from the topic you enter.

Each provider processes data under its own privacy terms.

5. Data retention

Account data and your synced content are retained while your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time (see “Your rights”).

6. Children’s privacy

Viska is a family-friendly game suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children for our own purposes. Children can play in guest mode without an account or any data collection. Account creation and purchases are intended to be performed by an adult. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us and we will delete it.

7. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal information, and to withdraw consent. To exercise any of these rights, or to request deletion of your account, email us at the address below and we will respond within a reasonable time.

8. International transfers

Our providers may process and store data in countries other than your own. Where required, we rely on appropriate safeguards for such transfers.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the “Last updated” date above. Material changes will be reflected here before they take effect.

10. Contact us

Questions about this policy or your data? Email fjordlabs.app@gmail.com.
